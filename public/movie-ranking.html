My Top 3 Movies Of All Time

Men of Honor

Cuba Gooding Jr did such an amazing job displaying courage and the will to fight by any means necessary. It's truly an inspiration to all who see it.

Self Made: Madam C. J. Walker

Another great inspirational story from someone that refuses to give up, even when life throws all kind of curve balls. I get pumped up excited watching this "limited series".

Titanic

Yes, it's true! I love this movie, even though it's 3 hours long! I could watch it again and again. Ever time I see itt, I cry! Like who doesn't like Titanic, right?
